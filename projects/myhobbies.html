My Hobbies

Basketball

My favorite sport is basketball. I don't know exactly why, besides the fact that my father likes it, too. My favorite NBA player is Kobe Bryant, because of the scoring ability he possessed. Fun fact: He's had more than 130 40-point games, an achievement most dream of. Also, he scored 81 points in one game, the second most by any player behind Wilt Chamberlain with 100. I don't just idolize NBA players. I play some basketball myself, usually as a small forward. I'm a decent shooter and rebounder, and my passing is okay.

Gaming

I definitely am a gamer. It's definitely my second favorite hobby, behind basketball. I've become quite good at it, too. Now, I usually play NBA 2K15 or Call of Duty: Black Ops 2/3 on the Xbox 360 or One, depending on if it's available for the One.

Listening to/Making Music

I am somewhat musically gifted. I can easily make a beat out of anything at any appropriate time. I also can sing or compose anything to an instrumental, if you send me one. It may take me a week or two, though, because I have homework and other things that need to be addressed.
